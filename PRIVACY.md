# Privacy Policy — legal-docs-mcp

**Last updated:** 2026-03-25

## 1. Data Controller

MCP Tools Lab — [https://github.com/mcp-tools-lab/legal-docs-mcp](https://github.com/mcp-tools-lab/legal-docs-mcp)
Email: contact@legal-docs-mcp.dev

## 2. Data Collected

The `legal-docs-mcp` MCP server processes the following data locally on your machine:

- **Input parameters:** Company information, client details, and document configuration you provide when calling tools. This data is processed in-memory to generate documents and is not transmitted to any external server.
- **Usage counters:** An anonymous in-memory counter tracks the number of documents generated per session for free tier limits. No personal data is stored persistently.

## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal data
- We do not use cookies or tracking
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- We do not store generated documents
- We do not log tool invocations

## 4. Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. The Service runs entirely on your local machine via the MCP protocol.

## 5. Data Retention

All data is processed in-memory only. No data persists after the MCP server process ends.

## 6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Since we do not collect or store personal data, these rights are satisfied by design.

## 7. Security

The Service runs locally on your machine. Data security depends on your local environment.

## 8. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected in the repository.

## 9. Contact

For privacy questions: contact@legal-docs-mcp.dev
